Emerald Cove Beach: Experience luxury and relaxation at the Taniti Beachfront Resort. Nestled along the pristine coastline, this 5-star resort offers spacious ocean-view rooms, private beach access, and a range of amenities including spa services and gourmet dining.

Guests can unwind by the infinity pool, enjoy water sports, and savor fresh seafood at the resort's beachfront restaurant. It's the perfect choice for a romantic getaway or a rejuvenating retreat.

Seaside Bungalows: For a more intimate and rustic experience, consider staying in one of Taniti's seaside bungalows. These cozy accommodations offer direct access to the beach, allowing you to wake up to the sound of the waves.

Bungalows provide a sense of seclusion while still being close to local attractions. Enjoy beach bonfires, stargazing, and the soothing ambiance of the ocean.

Taniti Eco-Lodges: For nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers, Taniti National Park is a must-visit destination. Spanning diverse ecosystems, it offers a range of hiking trails and outdoor activities.

Visitors can trek through lush rainforests, spot unique wildlife, and discover hidden waterfalls. The park also provides opportunities for birdwatching, camping, and eco-friendly guided tours, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism.

Private Condos and Villas: If you prefer a home-away-from-home experience, private condos and villas are available across Taniti. These fully furnished accommodations offer spacious living areas and kitchens.

Enjoy the privacy and flexibility of self-catering, while still having access to resort-like amenities such as pools and gyms. Perfect for families or extended stays.

Island-Style Bed & Breakfasts: Experience Tanitian hospitality at island-style bed & breakfasts. These charming and cozy accommodations are often run by locals, offering a warm and personalized stay.

Wake up to homemade breakfasts featuring local ingredients, and receive insider tips on hidden gems and cultural experiences from your hosts.

Luxury Treetop Retreat: For a truly unique experience, consider a stay in a luxury treetop retreat. These elevated accommodations provide panoramic views of the rainforest canopy.

Guests can enjoy the tranquility of the treetops, spot exotic wildlife from their balconies, and unwind in private hot tubs or outdoor baths.
