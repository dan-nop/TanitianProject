Hello!

Welcome to Taniti, the hidden gem of the Pacific! Planning your journey to our paradise is an exciting step toward experiencing our beautiful island. Below, we provide comprehensive information on the various ways to get to Taniti.

By Air:

Airport: Taniti International Airport (TIA)
  • Description: Taniti International Airport is the main gateway to our island. It welcomes domestic and international flights, making it a convenient choice for travelers from around the world.
  • Domestic Flights: Major airlines operate domestic flights to TIA from several regional airports.
  • International Flights: TIA is well-connected to international destinations, with direct flights from major cities.

    • By Sea:

    Cruise Ships:
  • Description: Cruise ships frequently dock at Taniti's picturesque harbor. Cruising to Taniti allows you to enjoy stunning coastal views as you approach the island.
  • Cruise Lines: Several cruise lines offer itineraries that include Taniti as a port of call.

    • By Land:

    Island Transportation:
  • Car Rentals: Exploring Taniti at your own pace is easy with car rentals available at the airport and in major towns.
  • Public Transportation: Public buses and taxis are readily available for convenient island travel.
  • Shuttle Services: Many accommodations offer shuttle services to and from the airport, ensuring a hassle-free arrival.
